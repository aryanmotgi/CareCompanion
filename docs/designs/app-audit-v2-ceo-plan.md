## Vision

### 10x Check
The original spec fixes 21 bugs. The 10x version asks: what would make CareCompanion the app that every oncology nurse recommends to patients on day one of diagnosis?

Three pillars:
1. **Guided onboarding** that holds your hand through the first week
2. **Smart timeline** that tells you what matters right now
3. **Treatment cycle awareness** that makes the AI companion genuinely useful

### Platonic Ideal
You open the app. It knows where you are in your treatment cycle. It tells you what matters today. It remembers everything your oncologist said last visit. It alerts your family when something changes. It generates a perfect health summary when you walk into the ER at 2am. Zero cognitive load during the hardest time of your life.
