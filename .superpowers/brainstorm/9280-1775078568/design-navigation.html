How it works

  • Bottom tab bar (4 tabs) — Home, Chat, Care, Scan. Always visible. This is the primary nav.
  • Top bar — App name on left, profile avatar on right. Tapping avatar opens a slide-out menu with: Care Profile, Connected Accounts, Settings.
  • Sidebar removed entirely — No hamburger menu, no collapsible sidebar. Bottom tabs only.
  • Care tab — Combines Medications + Appointments into one screen with a toggle/segment control at the top.
  • Scan tab — Opens directly to the camera/scan interface. One tap to scan.
