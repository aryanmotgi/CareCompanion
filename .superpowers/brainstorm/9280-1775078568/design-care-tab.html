How it works

  • Segment control — Toggle between Medications and Appointments at the top. No page navigation needed.
  • Grouped by status — Meds that need refills surface first. Active meds below. No scrolling past irrelevant stuff.
  • "+" button — Top right adds a new medication or appointment depending on which segment is active.
  • Tap to expand — Tapping a med card opens a detail sheet (dosage, pharmacy, doctor, history) as a bottom sheet overlay, not a new page.
  • Appointments view — Same card style but grouped by "This Week" and "Upcoming". Shows doctor name, specialty, date/time.
