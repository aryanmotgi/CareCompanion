Three approaches to reorganize CareCompanion

All keep your dark color palette. The difference is how aggressively we restructure.

A

Light Touch — Clean up, don't restructure

Keep all 8 pages. Add a mobile bottom tab bar for the top 4 (Home, Chat, Care, Scan). Simplify dashboard layout. Fix spacing and hierarchy. Tuck profile/settings/connect behind a menu.

Effort: Low  |  Risk: Low  |  Impact: Medium
B

Recommended — Mobile-first rebuild of navigation & layout

4-tab bottom nav (Home, Chat, Care, Scan). Merge medications + appointments into a single "Care" tab with sub-sections. Dashboard becomes a focused home feed with cards. Profile/Connect/Settings behind avatar menu. Remove sidebar entirely. Every screen = one clear purpose.

Effort: Medium  |  Risk: Low-Med  |  Impact: High — feels like a real app
C

Full Rethink — Chat-first architecture

Make AI Chat the home screen. Everything else (meds, appointments, scans) is accessible through chat or via a minimal secondary nav. Like how ChatGPT is chat-first with tools built in. Dashboard becomes a widget inside chat context.

Effort: High  |  Risk: High  |  Impact: High but polarizing
