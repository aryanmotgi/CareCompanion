How Manual FHIR Works — Simple Version

3 steps. One file. Profile fully populated.

1

Patient logs into their hospital portal

Epic MyChart, Kaiser, UCLA Health, Cerner — whatever their doctor uses. They already have an account. This is where they view their test results and appointments.

💡 Most cancer patients already use MyChart to see their lab results
2

They tap "Download My Health Data"

Every US hospital portal has this option — it's legally required. Usually under Menu → Health → Download My Data. Takes about 30 seconds. Downloads as a .json file.

💡 CareCompanion shows them exactly where to click with screenshots for their specific portal
3

They upload the file to CareCompanion

Drag & drop. We parse it in seconds. Everything auto-fills.

What CareCompanion auto-fills from that one file

💊 All medications
name, dose, frequency
🩺 Doctors
name, specialty, contact
🔬 Lab results
values, dates, trends
🏥 Diagnoses
cancer type, stage, conditions
⚠️ Allergies
drug & food allergies
📋 Procedures
surgeries, treatments
The only thing users type: their name, their relationship to the patient, and confirm what got imported. That's it.
